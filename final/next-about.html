Your position

The date is September 21, 2022. You are a border officer of Moralis, a fictional country located at the north of Russia. Following the Russian's invasion of Ukraine and their partial mobilization, there has been an influx of Russian tourists and citizen coming in Moralis.

Your Job

Verify and discrepancies in their documents and decide whether or not they are allowed entry into the country. Follow protocol, or you will be punished.

About the Project

Purpose

This web interactive fiction reframes the Ukraine war from the perspective of Russian citizens. Are they cowards for fleeing the country, or are they victims to Russia dictatorship and propaganda? Many who fled the country were against the war, but felt powerless to have any influence on it. Stuck between the Kremlin regime and the rejection of the world, where can they turn to for help?

So far, Poland, Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania and Poland have closed their border to Russian tourists who out of concern for security threats.
